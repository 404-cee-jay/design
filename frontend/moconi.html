Brand Design Case Study

MOCONI

A visual identity study that brings structure, polish, and flexibility to the Moconi brand system.

About the brand

Moconi needed a brand presence that felt modern and dependable while still leaving room for a more expressive visual tone.

The identity was shaped to be easy to recognise, easy to deploy, and strong enough to work across digital content, posters, and campaign graphics.

The solution

What solved the brief

A balanced layout approach with stronger hierarchy and a cleaner presentation style that makes the brand easier to apply consistently.

The benefits

What it gives the brand

Moconi gets a clearer identity, stronger recognition, and a visual language that can scale across future brand materials.
